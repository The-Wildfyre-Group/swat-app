Our Call-to-Action.

We promote accountability of the the American justice system by responding to and building on the recommendation of the Presidents' Task Force of 21st Century Policing.

Pillar One: Building Trust & Legitimacy 1.7.1 Action Item: “The Federal Government should develop survey tools and instructions for [tracking the level of trust in police] to prevent local departments from incurring the expense and to allow for consistency across jurisdictions” (16).

The SWAT app surveys the experience of residents following an encounter with police.

Pillar Two: Policy & Oversight 2.6.1 Action Item: “The Federal Government could further incentivize universities and other organizations to partner with police departments to collect data and develop knowledge about analysis and benchmarks as well as to develop tools and templates that help departments manage data collection and analysis” (25).

The content from SWAT app is used to populate the Database of police performance. Law enforcement agencies will use it to develop scenario-based training and influence nationwide curricula.
